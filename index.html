About

Us

Deepak Cards is the reputed wedding card Studio situated in the heart of Gwalior, for over 40 years. Deepak Cards has concept to creation of the wedding cards within specified timeframe delivery of the finished product is a speciality, due to excellent in-house production facility. We have a team of artists and visualisers to cater the specific requirements of their esteemed clients and tailor the cards to the minutest details for the memoire of their lifetime. The wedding cards presented in gold-foiling and silk-screen-raised printings is our specaility. We also undertake the printing of commercial jobs like Prospectus, Brochures, Catalogue, Magazines, Letterheads, Visiting cards, books and periodicals, publicity folders etc.
